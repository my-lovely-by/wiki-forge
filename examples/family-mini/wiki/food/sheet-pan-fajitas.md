# Sheet-pan fajitas

The kids will eat anything with tortillas.

## Ingredients

- 1 lb chicken breast, sliced
- 2 bell peppers, sliced
- 1 onion, sliced
- Fajita seasoning
- 8 small tortillas

## Method

Toss everything except tortillas with oil and seasoning, roast at 425°F for 20 minutes.
